About Mantooth

My Window to the World

Our Story

Mantooth was started as a way to showcase and create a working portfolio of my writing. My blogs are also a way of sharing with others what I find most worthy to write about and to share my insights on the world and culture that surrounds me.

Our Philosophy

Digital Presence

With the ever so changing world of technology, it was about time that I secured my spot in the "world wide web" to broadcast the world through my eyes.

Documentation

More than ever, I find the need to document and to write about things, places, people and activities that bring me joy.

Connection

I hope that by creating a space to share my thoughts and experiences, that it might relate to others. I wish to bring smiles, laughs and possibly spark new perspectives on things. So thanks for clicking and I hope you enjoy!

Meet the Author

My name is Ella and I am a millennial, born and raised in Phoenix AZ, but an aspiring Pacific Northwest dweller! I live with the most wonderful boyfriend in the whole wide world and our four cats.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, watching movies in the theater, drawing, hiking, writing poetry and snapping my polaroids. I am also known for rocking out in my car and sipping craft cocktails with good company.
